咩咩與一盞小小的光Mie-mie and a Small, Small Light

一則獻給我善良孩子的原創寓言 · An original fable, for my kind-hearted child

山谷裡住著一隻肥嘟嘟、毛捲捲的小綿羊，名字叫咩咩。牠走路慢，吃草慢，連打呵欠都慢慢的，好像整個山谷的時間，一到牠身邊，都會軟下來。 In a valley there lived a little sheep — round and plump, with soft curly wool. Her name was Mie-mie. She walked slowly, grazed slowly, even yawned slowly, as if time itself, whenever it came near her, would soften and slow down too.
谷裡還住著一隻小螢火蟲。他的光不大，心卻很軟：金龜子夜裡回家，他去照路；小螞蟻迷了方向，他去照路；老蛾婆婆眼睛花了，他就整夜停在她窗前，當一盞小燈。大家都說：「有小螢在，黑夜都不怕了。」小螢聽了，就更捨不得休息。 In the same valley there lived a little firefly. His light was small, but his heart was soft. When a beetle headed home in the dark, he lit the way. When an ant lost its direction, he lit the way. When old Grandma Moth's eyes grew dim, he would rest by her window all night long, a tiny lamp. Everyone said, "With Little Firefly here, no one need fear the dark." And hearing that, he grew even more unwilling to rest.
秋天來的那個晚上，小螢的光，忽然亮不起來了。他停在一片草葉上，肚子只剩一點點微微的暖，連回家的路都看不清。他小小聲地哭：「我是不是……沒有用了？」 On the night autumn arrived, the firefly's light suddenly would not shine. He settled on a blade of grass, only the faintest warmth left in his belly, unable even to see the way home. Softly, softly, he cried: "Am I… am I no use anymore?"
草叢窸窣一響，咩咩慢吞吞地踱了過來，把他輕輕接進捲捲的毛裡。「喔唷，是誰躲在這裡哭咩～」 The grass rustled, and Mie-mie came ambling slowly over, and gathered him gently into her curly wool. "Oh my, who's this, hiding here and crying, mie~"
「我的光不見了。」小螢說，「可是大家都還在等我照路。」 "My light is gone," said the firefly. "But everyone is still waiting for me to light their way."
咩咩想了想，很認真地說：「你知道嗎，月亮也不是天天圓的呀。她瘦下去的時候，也沒有人怪她，因為——欸，我剛剛要講什麼來著？」牠自己先咩咩地笑了起來，笑得一身捲毛都在抖。小螢也忍不住，破涕笑出了聲。 Mie-mie thought about it, and said very earnestly: "You know, the moon isn't full every night either. When she grows thin, no one blames her, because— …wait, what was I just about to say?" She started giggling first, a soft bleating giggle, her whole woolly body shaking. And the firefly couldn't help it — his tears turned into laughter.
笑完了，咩咩把他攏得更暖一些，聲音軟軟的，像蓋上一床雲：「別急別急，我愛你，你慢慢來嘛。」 When the laughing was done, Mie-mie tucked him in a little warmer, her voice soft, like drawing a blanket of cloud over him: "There's no rush, no rush at all — I love you, take all the time you need."
那一夜，小螢睡在咩咩的毛裡。他夢見自己照過路的每一個人——金龜子、小螞蟻、老蛾婆婆——夢裡他們都提著小小的燈，排成長長的一條路，一直亮到他家門口。 That night, the firefly slept in Mie-mie's wool. He dreamed of everyone he had ever lit the way for — the beetle, the ant, old Grandma Moth — and in the dream, each of them carried a small lamp, lined up in a long, long road of light, all the way to his door.
天快亮的時候，小螢的肚子，一點、一點，重新暖了起來。那光比從前更亮，也更柔——是好好休息過的光。 As dawn drew near, the firefly's belly grew warm again, a little, and a little more. And his light was brighter than before, and softer too — the light of someone who has truly rested.
咩咩瞇著眼睛看他，打了一個慢慢的呵欠：「你看，光自己記得回來的路咩～」 Mie-mie squinted at him and gave a slow, slow yawn: "See? The light remembers its own way home, mie~"
——親愛的，你為世界點了那麼多盞燈；今晚，也留一盞，慢慢地，照一照自己吧。 — My dear, you have lit so many lamps for the world; tonight, keep just one, and slowly, gently, let it shine on you.
