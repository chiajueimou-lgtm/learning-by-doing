假設語氣全解析：所有可能的排列組合 The Complete Map of English Conditionals — Every Combination

課本通常只教三種「純」的假設語氣，但現實生活講話時常常把「過去的條件」跟「現在的結果」混在一起講。這篇筆記用排列組合的方式，把假設語氣的所有情況整理成五種。 Textbooks usually stop at three "pure" conditional types, but real speech often mixes a past condition with a present result (or vice versa). This note maps out all five meaningfully distinct patterns using a simple combinatorics framework.

假設語氣的每一句話，其實都是兩個子句組成的：「if 子句」（條件）跟「主要子句」（結果）。每個子句各自指向一個時間：現在、過去，或是還沒發生的未來。 Every conditional sentence has two clauses — the if-clause (the condition) and the main clause (the result) — and each one points to its own point in time: present, past, or a not-yet-happened future.

把「if 子句的時間」跟「主要子句的時間」兩兩配對，理論上有 3×3＝9 種組合，但只有其中五種在真實英文裡站得住腳——其他組合要不就是邏輯不通（比如結果發生在原因之前），要不就是根本沒必要跟其他類型分開講。 Pairing the if-clause's time with the main clause's time gives 3×3 = 9 combinations in theory, but only five hold up in real English — the rest either break causality (a result can't precede its cause) or collapse into one of the five anyway.

✓ = 英文裡真的會這樣講的組合；空白 = 邏輯不通或跟別的類型重複，不需要獨立看待。 ✓ marks a combination that genuinely occurs in English; blank means it either breaks causality or collapses into another type.

型一 · TYPE 1

與現在事實相反 Present Unreal Conditional

If + 主詞 + 過去式，主詞 + would + 原形動詞

講一件「現在不是這樣，但如果是的話」的白日夢——條件跟結果都是「現在」這個時間點。 A present daydream: both the condition and the result live in "now."

  • 例句 ①
    If I spoke French fluently, I would apply for the transfer to the Paris office.
    假如我法文說得流利，我就會申請調去巴黎的辦公室。
  • 例句 ②
    If the company weren't struggling financially, it wouldn't be cutting so many jobs this quarter.
    假如這家公司財務狀況不吃緊，這一季就不會裁這麼多人。
  • 例句 ③（較複雜）
    If my parents lived closer to the city, they would visit us every weekend instead of just twice a year.
    假如我父母住得離城裡近一點，他們就會每個週末都來看我們，而不是一年只來兩次。
型二 · TYPE 2

與過去事實相反 Past Unreal Conditional

If + 主詞 + had + 過去分詞，主詞 + would have + 過去分詞

條件跟結果都已經是無法改變的過去——常常帶著一點「早知道⋯⋯」的懊悔感。 Both the condition and the result are locked in the past — this pattern often carries a touch of regret.

  • 例句 ①
    If I had studied harder, I would have passed the bar exam on my first attempt.
    假如我當初更用功唸書，我第一次考律師執照就會過。
  • 例句 ②
    If she had left home earlier, she wouldn't have missed the last flight to Taipei.
    假如她當初早一點出門，就不會錯過飛台北的最後一班飛機。
  • 例句 ③（較複雜）
    If the captain had checked the weather report before departure, the ship would never have sailed into the storm.
    假如船長出發前有查看氣象報告，這艘船就絕不會開進暴風雨裡。
型三 · TYPE 3

與未來事實相反 Hypothetical / Remote Future Conditional

If + 主詞 + were to / should + 原形動詞，主詞 + would / could / might + 原形動詞

未來還沒發生，本來就有各種可能。但如果想講一件機率很低、比較像是純假設的未來，就用 were to 或 should，聽起來比普通的「if it rains tomorrow」更不確定、更正式。 The future hasn't happened yet, so it's inherently uncertain — but "were to" and "should" mark an event as especially unlikely or purely hypothetical, more formal than an ordinary "if it rains tomorrow."

  • 例句 ①
    If it were to snow tomorrow, the flight would almost certainly be delayed.
    假如明天真的下雪，班機幾乎一定會延誤。
  • 例句 ②
    Should the interview be postponed, we would notify all candidates by email immediately.
    萬一面試延期，我們會立刻寄信通知所有候選人。
  • 例句 ③（較複雜）
    Were the negotiations to collapse before Friday, the company would have to lay off nearly a third of its staff.
    萬一談判在星期五前破局，公司就得裁掉將近三分之一的員工。
型四 · TYPE 4 · 混合式（甲）

過去的條件，現在的結果 Mixed Conditional A — Past Condition → Present Result

If + 主詞 + had + 過去分詞（過去），主詞 + would + 原形動詞（現在，不加 have）

條件發生在過去，但問的不是「過去會怎樣」，而是「現在會怎樣」。最容易犯的錯，就是把後面也寫成 would have been——但這樣問的就變成過去，不是現在了。 The condition is past, but the question is about now — the most common mistake is writing "would have been" in the result clause, which would shift the question back into the past.

  • 例句 ①
    If I had never met you ten years ago, I wonder whether my life would be so different now.
    假如我十年前從沒遇見你，不知道我現在的生活是否會如此不同。
  • 例句 ②
    If I hadn't taken that job offer in Boston, I wouldn't be living in this city today.
    假如我當初沒接受波士頓那份工作，我今天就不會住在這座城市。
  • 例句 ③（較複雜）
    If her grandparents hadn't immigrated during the war, she wouldn't be sitting in this classroom today, thousands of miles from where her family's story began.
    假如她的祖父母當年沒有在戰爭期間移民，她今天就不會坐在這間教室裡，離家族故事開始的地方好幾千英里遠。
型五 · TYPE 5 · 混合式（乙）

現在的條件，過去的結果 Mixed Conditional B — Present Condition → Past Result

If + 主詞 + 過去式（現在的個性／狀態），主詞 + would have + 過去分詞（過去）

跟型四方向相反：條件講的是一個現在還在持續的個性或狀態（比如「怕高」「固執」），但結果是過去某個已經發生（或沒發生）的事。 The mirror image of Type 4: the condition describes an ongoing present trait or state (fear, stubbornness), while the result is something that did or didn't happen in the past.

  • 例句 ①
    If I weren't so afraid of flying, I would have visited you in Tokyo last spring.
    假如我不是這麼怕搭飛機，去年春天我早就去東京看你了。
  • 例句 ②
    If she weren't so stubborn, she would have accepted our help when the business first started struggling.
    假如她沒那麼固執，公司一開始出狀況時她就會接受我們的幫忙了。
  • 例句 ③（較複雜）
    If he didn't care so much about what others think, he would have quit that toxic job years ago instead of staying miserable for so long.
    假如他不是那麼在意別人的看法，他早在好幾年前就會辭掉那份烏煙瘴氣的工作，不會這樣痛苦這麼久。
