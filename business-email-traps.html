商業書信最容易踩的三個文法陷阱 Three Grammar Traps in Business Email Writing

這篇筆記的起點是一句我每天都在用、卻一直寫錯的開頭句：「Enclosed I'm sending you company calendar...」。拆開來看，背後其實是三個會反覆出現的固定陷阱——抓住這三個，以後同類的句子就能自己抓出問題。 This note started from a sentence I write every day, incorrectly: "Enclosed I'm sending you company calendar..." Underneath it are three recurring traps — once you know them, you can catch similar sentences yourself.

這不是語言學理論，是一份「寄出前檢查清單」——目標是讓每天寫的商業書信少犯這三種最常見、也最容易讓人尷尬的錯。 This isn't linguistic theory — it's a pre-send checklist, aimed at catching the three most common (and most embarrassing) mistakes in daily business email.

陷阱一 · DANGLING OPENERS

固定開頭片語的「懸垂修飾語」 Fixed Opener Phrases & Dangling Modifiers

商業書信有一小組固定的開頭片語（Enclosed、Attached、Regarding⋯），很多人是把它們當「模板」背下來直接套用，沒搞懂背後的文法邏輯，結果套錯了結構——這種字放在句首時，文法上應該修飾後面那個「東西」（附件、行事曆），不是修飾「我」。 Business emails rely on a small set of fixed openers (Enclosed, Attached, Regarding...) that get memorized as templates without understanding the grammar underneath — so they get slotted into the wrong structure. Placed at the start of a sentence, these words should grammatically modify the thing being attached, not "I."

  • ✗ 錯誤示範（懸垂修飾語）
    Enclosed I'm sending you the calendar.
    字面邏輯上，Enclosed 變成在修飾「I」——但「我」不是被附上的東西，是行事曆。
  • ✓ 正確 — 固定搭配
    Enclosed, please find the calendar.
    Enclosed 後面接祈使句 please find，這是固定搭配，加逗號。
  • ✓ 正確 — 表語倒裝
    Enclosed is the calendar for your review.
    Enclosed 當表語形容詞，主詞倒裝（相當於 "The calendar is enclosed"）。
  • ✓ 正確（較複雜，也是最推薦的現代寫法）
    I'm sending you the calendar, updated as of today, for your reference.
    乾脆不用 Enclosed，直接用 sending 當主要動詞——現代商業英文越來越少用 "Enclosed/Attached please find" 這種老派句型。
想用的開頭安全的固定搭配
EnclosedEnclosed, please find... / Enclosed is...
AttachedAttached is... / Please find the report attached.
不想用固定片語I'm sending you... / I've attached...（最推薦）
順便抓一個同一家族的高頻錯誤：Regarding 本身已經有「關於」的意思，後面不需要再加 to。「Regarding to your question」是錯的，要嘛寫 Regarding your question，要嘛寫 With regard to your question（regard 才配 to）。 A related high-frequency error: "regarding" already means "concerning" and never takes "to" after it. Write "regarding your question" or "with regard to your question" — never "regarding to."
陷阱二 · ARTICLES（中文母語者最頭痛）

冠詞 a / an / the / 不加冠詞 Articles: The Hardest Thing for Chinese Speakers

判斷法：讀者看到這個名詞，知不知道我講的「確切是哪一個」？

中文沒有冠詞系統，這是中文母語者寫英文最持續、最難根除的問題，沒有之一。好消息是商業書信裡的名詞多半是「雙方都知道是哪一個」的東西（那份報告、這次會議、這個行事曆），所以大部分時候答案是 the。 Chinese has no article system, making this the single most persistent issue for Chinese-speaking writers. The good news: business-email nouns are usually something both sides already know exactly which one — so most of the time, the answer is "the."

  • ✗ 錯誤示範
    Please review company calendar.
    公司只有一份行事曆，雙方都知道是哪一份，漏了冠詞。
  • ✓ 正確
    Please review the company calendar.
    特定、雙方都知道 → the。
  • ✗ 錯誤示範
    I have question about the report.
    「一個問題」是第一次提到、還不確定是哪個問題，不該用 the，但也不能不加冠詞。
  • ✓ 正確（較複雜）
    I have a question about the report you sent yesterday.
    「一個問題」是新資訊、不確定哪一個 → a；「你昨天寄的報告」雙方都知道是哪份 → the。同一句話裡兩種冠詞邏輯並存，很典型。
不需要冠詞的情況：講「不可數名詞」或「泛指、一般性」的東西時，直接不加冠詞。例如 "We need more information before proceeding."（information 不可數）、"Please provide feedback by Friday."（feedback 不可數，泛指）。 No article needed for uncountable or general/generic nouns — e.g. "more information," "provide feedback."
商業書信常見詞幾乎都用
report, meeting, deadline, budget, calendar, invoice, attachmentthe（幾乎都是特定、雙方已知的那一份）
question, issue, idea, concern（第一次提到、還不確定哪一個）a / an
information, feedback, advice, progress（不可數）不加冠詞
陷阱三 · INTRODUCTORY COMMA

句首引導詞後面要加逗號 Comma After Introductory Words

句首的單字／短語引導詞，後面加逗號

跟前面 Enclosed 的例子一樣，這條規則本身不難——只是常常跟其他問題（像懸垂修飾語）疊在一起，才顯得複雜。單獨看，規則很單純：句首放一個引導詞或短語，後面就加逗號。 This rule alone is simple — it's compounded when it overlaps with other issues (like the dangling modifier above). On its own: an introductory word or phrase at the start of a sentence gets a comma after it.

  • ✓ 正確
    However, the deadline has been moved to Friday.
    句首引導詞 However，後面加逗號。
  • ✓ 正確（較複雜）
    As requested in our last meeting, I've attached the updated budget.
    句首引導片語較長時，同樣規則適用，逗號放在片語結束的地方。
商業書信常見句首引導詞（後面都要加逗號）
However,Additionally,Furthermore,Enclosed,
As mentioned above,As requested,Per our conversation,Going forward,

📋 寄出前檢查清單 Pre-Send Checklist

  1. 句首有沒有 Enclosed / Attached / Regarding 這類字？接的結構對嗎？（還是乾脆改用 "I'm sending you..." 最保險） Does the opener match a safe fixed structure — or would "I'm sending you..." be simpler and safer?
  2. 每個名詞前面，讀者知不知道我講的確切是哪一個？知道 → the；不知道、第一次提 → a/an；泛指、不可數 → 不加。 For every noun: does the reader know exactly which one? Known → the; new/unspecified → a/an; generic/uncountable → no article.
  3. 句首有沒有引導詞（However、As requested、Enclosed⋯）？後面加逗號了嗎？ Is there an introductory word or phrase at the start — and does it have a comma after it?
